3 Minute Thesis

Communicating machine learning and vaccine response research to a broad audience in three minutes.

The Three Minute Thesis, originally developed at the University of Queensland, is an internationally recognized research communication competition that challenges doctoral candidates to present their work to a general audience in just three minutes.

I was selected as a finalist in Boston University’s 2026 3MT competition, representing one of ten PhD candidates invited to compete in the final round.

My presentation focused on my thesis work applying machine learning approaches to predict vaccine response using early immune signals to inform long-term protection.
